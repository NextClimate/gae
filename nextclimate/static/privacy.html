Our privacy policy

Any information you provide to us is used strictly to give you more accurate and personally relevant climate change information and energy saving opportunities. We will share this information to other people or parties only when you request that we do so. We do not use this information for any other purpose, and we protect your information so that it remains safe.

Please contact us with any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy at info@nextclimate.org

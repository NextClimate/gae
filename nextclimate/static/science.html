International effort, locally focused

As part of the Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change's Fourth Assessment Report, climate scientists from around the world contributed their results to a common data archive. These results have been re-analyzed based on local climate patterns. Finally, we map these local results to your zip code.

The data presented here are not a forecast of the future. This is impossible. Rather they are a warning of what will likely come to pass if we continue on our current course of action.
